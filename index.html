Category 01

Feeding & Weaning

Silicone sets · bibs · weaning utensils · toddler tableware

Nimova Opportunity: The mid-premium feeding segment on Noon UAE and Amazon.ae is dominated by generic, weakly-branded sets in oversaturated primary colours. No single brand owns a cohesive identity across set + bib. Nimova's SoftStart Weaning Set (AED 85–110) and CatchAll Silicone Bib 2-pack (AED 45–65) enter with gift-ready packaging, LFGB cert on the front panel, and a brand palette (Dusty Sage, Blush Mist, Dusk Blue) that stands apart from everything in-market.

Nimova Opportunity: The nursery caddy and storage basket segment on Amazon.ae is the single weakest branded category in UAE baby non-consumables. Nimova's NestCaddy (AED 75–95) and NestBasket Storage Set (AED 85–120 for 3-pc) can become the only branded mid-premium nursery storage line on the platform.
